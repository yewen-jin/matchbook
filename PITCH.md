# Pitch & Demo — 2 minutes

Judging is 50% "real problem + strong Xero use", so the **story carries the score**, not the visuals. Lead as the user, not the developer.

## Open (30s) — Caslean's story
My friend Caslean is a musician. She also runs a successful Depop shop, and models on the side. Her bookings arrive as promoter emails, agency DMs, and calls — and every one of them means paperwork she dreads: who do I invoice, for what, by when? So invoices go out late, or wrong, or never. She's brilliant at three things, and admin steals time from all of them. She can't afford an accounts department. So I built her one.

## Show (60s) — the loop, live
> "She got the gig. Watch the agent do the paperwork."

1. Paste a real-shaped booking conversation — an agency DM with a day rate and a usage fee.
2. The agent extracts the deal and **shows its working** — every field traced to the phrase it came from.
3. It resolves the client in **Xero**, checks she hasn't already invoiced this gig, and drafts the invoice as a diff of exactly what it will write.
4. **She taps approve** — the invoice is created in Xero, live. Show it in the sandbox, with the agent's reasoning written into the invoice history.
5. Paste the vague one — "usual rate?" — and the agent **asks instead of guessing**. That moment is the star.
6. (If Stretch 1 shipped:) the agent also spots her overdue invoice and drafts the chase — approved, sent, logged.

## Land (20s) — the trust line
Anyone can build an agent that talks. The hard part is one you'd trust near real money. This one shows its provenance, asks when it isn't sure, and never touches the ledger without sign-off — autonomous work, human approval, full audit trail.

## Close (10s)
> "From booking to correct books in one tap. Today she pastes; tomorrow it's forwarded emails and meeting transcripts. Caslean gets her hours back for the work she's actually good at — that's the whole point of the bounty."

## Submission description (draft)
An agent that turns booking conversations — emails, DMs, call transcripts — into correct Xero invoices. It extracts the deal with per-field provenance, resolves the contact, blocks duplicates, asks instead of guessing, and writes to Xero only after one-tap approval, logging every action and reason. Built for multi-hustle creatives like Caslean (musician, Depop seller, model) who lose hours to the admin between getting the gig and getting paid. Bounty 01.

## Notes
- Cue a **recorded backup clip** in case the live API flakes.
- Don't demo breadth — demo the **one loop**, flawlessly. The asks-instead-of-guessing beat is the trust feature.
- If asked "app or agent?": an agent does the work, in an app that makes it trustworthy.
- If asked "why paste, not email integration?": DMs and calls have no usable API; paste is channel-agnostic and true to her life. Forwarding addresses and transcript feeds are transport — the architecture takes text in, ledger actions out.
- If asked about tax: reconciled, correctly-invoiced books by income stream are the foundation; per-stream tax-prep breakdown is the roadmap.
